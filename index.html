Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our investment platform

How do I start investing?

Simply sign up for an account, complete the verification process, choose an investment plan, and make your first deposit. You can start with as little as $100.

Are the 50% weekly returns guaranteed?

While past performance is not a guarantee of future results, our investment strategies have consistently delivered 30-50% weekly returns for over 5 years. We employ risk management strategies to maintain stable returns.

How quickly can I withdraw my money?

Withdrawals are processed within 2-24 hours, depending on the withdrawal method. There are no lock-in periods - you can withdraw your funds anytime.

Is my investment secure?

Yes, we use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption, two-factor authentication, and store funds in segregated accounts. We're also registered and compliant with financial regulations.

What is the partnership program?

Our partnership program allows you to earn 15% commission on deposits made by people you refer to VAULT. There's no limit to how much you can earn through referrals.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. We only charge a 2% withdrawal fee to cover processing costs. All other features, including deposits and account management, are completely free.
